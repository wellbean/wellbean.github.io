We hope to bring a service which will smoothen
the transition from primary to secondary school

We bring a programme that will be individualised for each primary school,
focusing primarily on student wellbeing.

We are super passionate!

We are super passionate about the wellbeing of youth, with special focus on Year 6's. We believe that these students have to undergo an extrodinary amount of change in a very short duration and wish to assist them to the best of our ability.

Wellbeing is our top priority

We hope to empower Year 6 students to be able to be more self aware, self confident and self reflective as they journey through their transition from primary to secondary education. We believe this programme will equip students with the necessary skills to crush it in their transition.
