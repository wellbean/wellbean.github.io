What is WellBean?

WellBean is a programme that seeks to equip the young people of today with the knowledge and skills to not only assess their own wellbeing, but also the ability to help support and foster a supportive community envrionment.

We believe the best way to do this is to work with young students prior to entering high school through a 4 week programme filled with fun and interactive activities. The key pillars of our program are around building relationships, overcoming difficulties, self esteem and expressing feelings.

What We Do

Tailored Programme

We bring a programme tailored to match the needs and wants to each individual school.

Passionate Team

The WellBean team is extremely passionate about increasing mental health literacy.

Professionals Approved

All parts of our programme has undergone rigorous checks by mental health professionals.

This programme will better equip your students to have a smooth transition into high school.
